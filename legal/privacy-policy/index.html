Website Privacy Policy

renefarious.github.io Last updated: March 2026
This policy applies to the Renefarious Studios website only. For the RW Mod Editor Android app, see the App Privacy Policy.

1. Introduction

Renefarious Studios ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website located at renefarious.github.io (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit the Site, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

By accessing the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the Site.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

The Site does not include any forms, registration, or account systems. The only way we receive information you provide directly is if you send us an email via the support address listed on the Site.

When you email us at renefarious.dev@gmail.com, we collect your email address and the content of your message in order to respond to your enquiry.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

The Site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit, including but not limited to:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time of visit

This data is collected by GitHub Inc. and is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement. Renefarious Studios does not have direct access to this log data.

2.3 Cookies

The Site does not set any first-party cookies. GitHub Pages may set cookies as part of its hosting infrastructure; please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

3. How We Use Information

Any information received via email is used solely to respond to your enquiry or support request. We do not use it for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 GitHub Pages

This Site is hosted by GitHub Pages, a service of GitHub Inc. GitHub's infrastructure may process visitor data as part of delivering the Site. Please review GitHub's Privacy Statement for full details.

4.2 Google Fonts

This Site loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser fetches these fonts, Google may receive your IP address. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details. No other Google services are used on this Site.

4.3 No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics or advertising tracking services on this Site.

5. Data Retention

Email correspondence is retained only as long as necessary to resolve the enquiry or as required by applicable law. We do not retain email communications beyond their immediate purpose.

6. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the address below and we will promptly delete it.

7. Links to External Sites

The Site may contain links to external websites, including GitHub and Google Play. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their respective policies.

8. Security

While we take reasonable steps to protect any information you send us via email, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. The Site itself is static and does not store user data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
